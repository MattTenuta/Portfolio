A Little About Myself

About Me

Hi there, my name is Matthew Tenuta and I am 20 years old. I am an Interactive Media Design graduate from Fanshawe College. Coming out of college, I plan to become a graphic/motion designer as those were by far my favourite classes. I put a lot of time and effort into my work, whether its learning new skills, or perfecting that last detail, I will get it done to the best possible.

I am a driven individual who is always looking for new challenges. I have a strong work ethic, I enjoy working with others and I am always willing to lend a helping hand. I love to work in teams as I think it creates a better environment for ideas to grow into something amazing. I have a positive attitude and I am always looking for the good in every situation.
